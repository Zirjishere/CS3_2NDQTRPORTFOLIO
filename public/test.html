Why Friday Is The Best Day.

Friday is the best day of the week because it is the last day of the school for the week. It is the day where I can play games and watch videos without any guilt because I know that I have no school the next day. Though I have to do a lot of reqs on the weekend, that's why I love Friday because I can just relax and continue working the next day.
